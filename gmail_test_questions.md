## 💡 Tips for Testing

1. **Use Real IDs**: Replace `[ID]` with actual email IDs from your inbox
2. **Use Real Emails**: Replace `[email]` with actual email addresses
3. **Test Categories**: Try questions from each section above
4. **Vary Wording**: The same intent can be phrased multiple ways
5. **Check Responses**: Verify the correct tool was called and response makes sense
6. **Edge Cases**: Try invalid IDs, missing permissions, etc.

---

## ⚡ Quick Test Sequence (10 Questions)

If you want a quick validation, ask these in order:

1. "Show my recent emails"
2. "Show my unread messages" 
3. "Search emails about 'work'"
4. "Show emails from [your-email]"
5. "Star the first email"
6. "Draft an email to [email] about testing"
7. "Summarize my last email"
8. "Archive email [ID]"
9. "Add label 'Test' to email [ID]"
10. "Trash email [ID]"

---

**Note**: When testing, use `--verbose` mode in the test script to see which tool is being called and verify correct routing.
