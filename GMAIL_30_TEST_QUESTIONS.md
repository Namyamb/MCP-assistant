# 30 Natural Test Questions for Gmail MCP

## Reading & Context (1-5)
1. "Show me my recent emails"
2. "Check my unread messages"
3. "Show me the first email"
4. "Read the last email for me"
5. "Show emails from today"

## Search & Filter (6-10)
6. "Search for emails about 'budget'"
7. "Find emails from john@example.com"
8. "Show emails labeled 'Work'"
9. "Find emails from last week"
10. "Search for urgent emails"

## Sending & Drafting (11-15)
11. "Send an email to john@example.com saying I'll be late"
12. "Draft an email to my manager about the proposal"
13. "Send report.pdf to the team with attachment"
14. "Send my saved draft"
15. "Delete draft"

## Reply & Forward with Context (16-20)
16. "Reply to the first email saying thanks"
17. "Forward the last email to my manager"
18. "Reply all to the first email"
19. "Draft a reply to the last email in formal tone"
20. "Generate follow-up for the first email"

## Organization - Labels & Stars (21-25)
21. "Add label 'Work' to the first email"
22. "Star the last email"
23. "Mark the first email as read"
24. "Archive this email"
25. "Show all my Gmail labels"

## AI Analysis (26-28)
26. "Summarize the first email"
27. "Check urgency of the last email"
28. "Extract tasks from the first email"

## Compound Commands (29-30)
29. "Show unread and mark the first one as read"
30. "Get my starred emails and trash the first one"
